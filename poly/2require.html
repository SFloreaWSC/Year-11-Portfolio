Requirements



  • Body
    • Head
      • Blue eyes(model probably wont have detailed eyes)
      • Long curly brown hair tied back
      • Generic other features(nose, ears, ect)
    • Torso
      • Chest
      • Little over 2 heads' width at widest point
      • Below 2 heads' width at waist
    • Arms
      • Reaches down past hips
      • Hands with fingers like shape (aka: not just a blob)
      • Roughly 1/2 of a head's width

  • Acessories (mostly not heavily required for modelling)
    • Clothes
      • Knee length navy skirt
      • Black Mary Jane shoes
      • Knee high socks, white or black
      • Tan blazer with dark buttons
      • Button up shirt under blazer
    • Other (optional, like if more detailed modelling is done later)
      • Black/Dark gray or Brown hair tie
      • Handbag of some sort
      • Little personality pins (eg: A little animal pin because of an intrest in animals, or a cupcake for an interest in baking)
