End Users



My end users are people in their late teen years to earlier adult years, generally around 15-25 years of age. This is because I want my character to resemble a human like figure clearly, but not be super realistic in all aspects. I believe that the given age range of people appreciate human characters, but don't require the character to have features such as fingernails or detailed eyes. I also chose this age range as I feel my character could be used in a video game quite well, and roughy 59% of 15-25 year olds play video games.
