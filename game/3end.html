End Users



My end users are generally in the range of older teenagers to young adults, but even older adults may still be interested due to the older nature of text-based adventure games. Younger teenagers and children don’t require much thought as they would likely not be interested in such a simple game, considering the current game media that is circulated. Older teens are paid mind to as they are generally more mature and would be more interested in the simplicity of such games. Any possible users for the game would likely be the kind of people who have heard of such games or played them in the past, and so emulating the same feel as these older games was vital to interest said users.
